Saturday 11 November 2017

Meet at 10.00 am in the car park at the top of the hill just east of Dousland. We will walk up the route of the old Yelverton to Princetown railway, crossing the new Peekhill Bridge. We will stay on the old railway line until shortly after Ingra Tor, before turning east and then south-east past some hut circles to cross the main B3212 road. We will soon reach Black Tor and its impressive Logan Stone.

Dropping downhill towards the River Meavy, we will pick up the Devonport Leat at the point where an aqueduct carries it across the river. We will then follow the leat through the woods above Burrator Reservoir. Picking up the road for a short stretch below Leather Tor, we branch away briefly through the woods again and end with a short uphill climb via the road back to the car park. The route is relatively easy, although there is one steep downhill section.

Saturday 9 December 2017

We will set out from the China Clay quarries near Lee Moor. After heading along the edge of the quarry, we will climb up to shell top before heading across Hentor Warren to meet the River Plym. Following the leats above the river, we will return via Trowlesworthy Warren.

Saturday 13 January 2018

A slightly shorter route in case the weather is poor, we start from Norsworthy Bridge and head up Down Tor and along the stone row. After reaching Nun's Cross, we will follow the Devonport Leat to Crazywell Pool and return to the cars via Raddick Hill.

Saturday 10 February 2018

We will be heading down to Bodmin Moor for a walk which will start in the village of Minions. We will head up past the Cheesewring and on towards Kilmar Tor, before returning via the route of a dismantled railway and the stone circles of The Hurlers.

Saturday 10 March 2018

A coastal walk on the Rame Peninsula in Cornwall. Parking at Cawsand in the morning, we will follow the coast path to Rame Head before looping across country back to Cawsand for lunch. In the afternoon we will head in the other direction on the coast path towards Mount Edgcumbe, returning via Maker Church.

Saturday 14 April 2018

Parking near Lydford, we will head for the old tramway and follow it up past Great Nodden and up towards Gren Tor. After branching off for Great Links Tor, we will visit Bleak House and Chat Tor before returning via Brat Tor and Widgery Cross.
